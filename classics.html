Classic Bindings

Classic Bindings are for those who want a tight, secure feel to their board. Being a little cheaper, it's hard to beat the quality and reliability of a good ole classic binding.

Union Ultra

These bindings that are priced at $299.95 which is about the average, but the overal quality is outstanding and it's main focus, along with quality and aesthetics. It has four primary colorways, good to match any outfit you plan on wearing!

Bent Metal

Bent Metal's Solution Snowboard Bindings are amongst some of the most expensive bindings on the market priced at $459.99. The price goes to it's build quality, as it features carbon highbakcs, baseplates, and forged aluminum buckles for extra reinforcement.

Union Flight

These are rated as some of the best budget bindings one can find. At the low price of $179.95, the Union Flight Pro offers a flexable build that allows for a very responsive feel. They offer many different colorways with very vibrant colors.

Cartel Re:Flex

The Cartel Re:Flex by Burton is priced at an average $279.95 and ensures great quality. Given the name, these bindings are meant to give the rider a lightweight, responsive feel as they are going down the slopes.
